Where & how to get in contact,
shared securely and privately.

Empowering you to come together with people that matter.

Available from your favorite app store starting in summer 2026...

Reunicorn is a smartphone app to share contact details, where you live and where you'll visit, all in a secure and privacy preserving manner, for everyone who wants to keep in touch with their peers.
When you are traveling, find out immediately who of your friends and acquaintances you could meet where, and directly reach out to them with up-to-date contact details via their preferred communication channel.
